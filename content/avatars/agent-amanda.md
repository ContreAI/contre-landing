## Notes for Content Creation

When writing for Agent Amanda:
- Lead with the problem she's feeling right now
- Keep paragraphs short (mobile reading)
- Include actionable takeaways she can use today
- Use scenarios she recognizes ("You're juggling 4 deals and...")
- Reference the commission/income angle
- Make her feel smart and capable, not behind
- Social proof from other agents is powerful
- End with something free and immediately useful
